    About Us

    PinkBall was incorporated in 2018 to champion innovative ideas using market intelligence and insights that will deliver on a consistent basis, enriching experiences to consumers and Clients’ brands. We are an Agency with a rich repertoire of experienced Specialists in brand building and well-trained cum highly motivated staff with strong passion for top notch service delivery. Our ethos is hinged on Integrity, Speed and Professionalism. We offer through-the-line (360-degree) marketing communications solutions.

    Our Mission

    Use market intelligence and insights to deliver higher engagement on a consistent basis, enriching experiences between consumers and clients' brands

    Our Core Values

    Open, Collaborative, Simple, Agile & Nimble, Interactive, Delightful, Resourceful

    Our Vision

    To become Africa’s Number one Creative Ideas and Brand Solutions Hub by 2025.

    THE CEO

    STANLEY IKPEMOSI SALAMI – (B.Sc Economics, MBA Marketing, ARPA)

    A thorough-bred adman and accomplished Marketing Communication's Strategist with over 25 years cognate experience working on a broad spectrum of communication campaigns (brand, corporate and political).

    He has strong analytical mind and strength in human psychology, which he applies effectively over the years, helping to build strong iconic brands in Nigeria such as United Bank for Africa, Toyota, Medview Airlines, First Bank, Glo Telecoms, Vmobile Telecoms now Airtel, Cadbury’s Bournvita, Dano Milk, Dunlop range of tyres, Seaman’s Schnapps and so on.

    Stanley’s life philosophy is “there’s no limit for the human mind”.

    OTHER SENIOR MANAGEMENT TEAM'S

    OLUSEGUN OYEYIPO – Art Director

    A graduate of Yaba College of Technology (YabaTech). An astute illustrator, a highly resourceful and deeply conceptual person. He started out as a Graphic Artist with TBWA Concept where he got involved in the creation of many great campaigns, which included the very popular Indomitables.


    At Rosabel Advertising Agency he was involved in the designs of Grand Oak Limited Products such as Seaman’s Schnapps, Lord’s Dry Gin and Dark Sailor Rum. He also created Designs for Samsung, Med-View Airline, Dano Milk and TomTom Confectionery.

    He is well trained locally and internationally.
